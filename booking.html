Jakarta Fair

Jakarta Fair, or Jakarta International Expo (JIExpo), is Indonesia's largest annual event, held from June to July. It features a wide range of products from various industries, including automotive, electronics, and fashion. The fair offers entertainment, cultural performances, concerts, and amusement rides, making it a vibrant celebration for both locals and tourists. It promotes local businesses and showcases Indonesia's rich diversity.
